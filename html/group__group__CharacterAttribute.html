Detailed Description

A Character has no control over the Attributes that they have but Attribute can have an affect on everything they do. Attributes are acquired as Skills are learned. And even if a Skill is lost to the Character, the Attributes from that Skill will remain.

This group of methods allow you to access the Health object.
